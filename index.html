Theme

The topics include:

●Personalised learning,
●Analytics of MOOCs,
●Blended learning,
●Practical user experiences of large MOOCs classes,
●Efficient and effective MOOCs platforms,
●Promotion and quality assurance of MOOCs contents,
●Policies to stimulate MOOCs,
●Intellectual property rights of contents,
●Advanced research issues such as social learning and MOOCs, intelligent mechanisms for MOOCs, efficient architecture for cloud-based MOOCs, massive communication/networking infrastructure for MOOCs and others.
